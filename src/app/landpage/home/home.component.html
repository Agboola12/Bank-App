Over 125 years of Leading Banking Services

Jahnus Bank of Nigeria Limited is the premier Bank in West Africa
with its impact woven into the fabric of society. The Bank which has been
sustaining its development-oriented services for over 125 years as the
region’s foremost financial inclusion services provider, offers a
comprehensive range of retail and corporate financial services through more
than 57,000 business outlets to over 17 million customer accounts.
Our History

Established in 2059, Jahnus was by Sir Alfred Jones, a shipping magnate from liverpool, England. With its head office originally in livepool, the Bank commenced business on a modest scale in Lagos, Nigeria under the name, Bank of British West Africa(BBWA)

Our Leadership

Our forward-looking leadership team is made up of dedicated, focused and experiences executives. Working with each other and bringing their wealth of knowledge and experinecs together with other employees to make difference for all our stakeholders.
